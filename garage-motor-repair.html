Signs You Need Garage Opener Repair

Don't wait until you're stuck in your garage. Look out for these common garage opener and motor issues.

Grinding or Loud Noises

If your motor sounds like it's struggling, grinding, or rattling, internal gears may be stripped or the chain/belt needs adjustment.

Motor Hums, Door Won't Move

You press the button, the motor hums, but nothing happens. This often indicates a burnt-out capacitor, a broken trolley, or stripped gears.

Intermittent Operation

Does the door stop halfway? Does it reverse for no reason? This could be a logic board issue, sensor misalignment, or force setting errors.
